Very brief modules/features

  • Frame scoreboard for two players.
  • Break tracker and turn switching.
  • Simple pot/foul actions based on snooker order.
  • Compact rules helper for what to score next.

Simple rules used here

  • Start with 15 reds. Potting a red gives 1 point and then a color is expected.
  • While reds remain, colors score and are re-spotted, then play returns to a red.
  • After all reds are gone, colors are potted in order: yellow, green, brown, blue, pink, black.
  • Fouls award 4, 5, 6, or 7 points to the opponent.
